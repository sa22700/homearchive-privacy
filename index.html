Privacy Policy for HomeArchive

Effective date: 2026-03-26

Developer:PiTo Tech

Contact: pito.tech.one@gmail.com

HomeArchive is an application for storing information about personal items, warranties, receipts, reminders, and related images. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Information processed by the app

HomeArchive may process information that you choose to add into the app, including:

  • product information such as name, brand, model, category, location, purchase date, price, serial number, notes, and warranty details
  • reminder information such as title, due date, repeat interval, and notes
  • product images
  • receipt images
  • local license and trial status related to Pro access

2. Camera permission

HomeArchive requests camera permission only so that you can take photos of products or receipts inside the app. The camera is opened only when you choose to use the camera feature. If camera permission is denied, the app will not access the camera.

3. Photo and file access

You may either:

  • take a new photo with your device camera, or
  • choose an existing image file from your device

Selected or captured images may be attached to product and receipt entries in the app.

4. How your data is stored

HomeArchive stores app data locally on your device. This includes a local SQLite database for items and reminders, a local image folder for saved product and receipt images, and a local license state file used for trial and Pro status.

5. How your data is used

Your information is used only to provide the app’s core features, such as:

  • storing and organizing your items
  • saving product and receipt images
  • calculating warranty information
  • creating reminders
  • generating local exports and reports
  • managing trial and Pro access status

6. Exports

HomeArchive allows you to export your data as CSV, JSON, and PDF files. These exports are created only when you request them.

7. Payments and Pro access

HomeArchive includes optional Pro functionality through an in-app purchase flow for a non-consumable product. If you choose to purchase Pro, payment processing may be handled by the relevant app store provider under its own terms and policies. HomeArchive does not need to store your full payment card information.

8. Data sharing

HomeArchive does not automatically share your stored item data, reminders, or images with third-party servers based on the current app functionality. Data may leave the app only if you choose to export it yourself or if future versions of the app add online features.

9. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or remove exported files yourself. You can also delete individual items and reminders inside the app.

10. Children’s privacy

HomeArchive is not specifically directed to children. The app is intended for general consumer use related to item and receipt management.

11. Security

HomeArchive stores data locally on your device. However, no app can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your device secure and controlling who has access to it.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the app’s data practices change, this policy should also be updated.
